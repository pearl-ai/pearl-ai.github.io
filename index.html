About

P.E.A.R.L. is an online DIY platform.

Understand Reinforcement Learning through slideshows, at your own pace.

Slideshows meant to provide better intuitions, using illustrations.

Coding assignments associated with every chapter.

Completely open-source.

Feel free to contribute and improve the understandability of the content, add new topics, improve the code, etc.
